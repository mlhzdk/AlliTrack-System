GSM Alarm System Troubleshooting & Maintenance Guide

Step-by-Step Troubleshooting

Follow these steps to identify and resolve common GSM Alarm System issues.

1. Alarm Not Triggering

  1. Step 1: Ensure sensors are powered and properly connected to the control panel.
  2. Step 2: Verify sensor zones are correctly configured in the system settings.
    3. ⚡Tip: Test each sensor individually to ensure proper functionality.

2. False Alarms

  1. Step 1: Check for environmental factors causing triggers (pets, vibration, etc.).
  2. Step 2: Adjust sensitivity settings for sensors prone to false alarms.
  3. Step 3: Ensure all tamper switches are properly installed and not loose.

3. SMS / Notification Not Sent

  1. Step 1: Verify GSM SIM card is active and has sufficient balance.
  2. Step 2: Ensure correct phone numbers are entered in the system.
  3. Step 3: Check signal strength at the device location.

4. Remote Arm / Disarm Not Working

  1. Step 1: Confirm remote access credentials are correct.
  2. Step 2: Ensure the device has network or GSM connectivity.
  3. Step 3: Restart the control panel if commands are not received.

5. Battery / Power Issues

  1. Step 1: Check battery level and replace if necessary.
    2. ⚡Tip: Use only manufacturer-approved batteries to avoid malfunctions.
  2. Step 2: Verify AC power supply is stable and connected.
  3. Step 3: Inspect wiring for damage or loose connections.
