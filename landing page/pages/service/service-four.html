Biometrics / Door Access Troubleshooting & Maintenance Guide

Step-by-Step Troubleshooting

Follow these steps to identify and resolve common access control issues.

1. Fingerprint / Card Not Recognized

  1. Step 1: Ensure the finger/card is properly enrolled in the system.
  2. Step 2: Clean the scanner surface to remove dirt or smudges.
    3. ⚡Tip: Re-enroll fingerprints if recognition fails repeatedly.

2. Door Unlock / Lock Fails

  1. Step 1: Verify the door strike or magnetic lock is powered and functioning.
  2. Step 2: Ensure wiring between controller and lock is intact.
  3. Step 3: Confirm access permissions are configured correctly.

3. System Not Responding

  1. Step 1: Check power supply and battery status.
  2. Step 2: Restart the controller if the system is frozen.
  3. Step 3: Verify network connectivity for networked access devices.

4. Enrollment Errors

  1. Step 1: Ensure the scanner is clean and functioning.
  2. Step 2: Verify that the user ID is not duplicated in the system.
  3. Step 3: Retry enrollment after correcting any errors.

5. Event Logs Not Updating

  1. Step 1: Check storage capacity for logs in the controller or server.
  2. Step 2: Ensure proper synchronization with monitoring software.
    3. ⚡Tip: Regularly export and backup logs to prevent data loss.

Still unable to resolve the issue?

Submit a ticket to the support team for advanced diagnostics and assistance.
