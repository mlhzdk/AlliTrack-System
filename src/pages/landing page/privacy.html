Privacy & Data Protection

PRIVACY NOTICE v2.3 AlliTrack intelligent surveillance Updated 15 March 2025

This privacy statement explains how AlliTrack handles personal data in connection with IP surveillance systems, cloud recordings, and support interactions — aligned with GDPR, CCPA and industry best practices.

What we collect & why

Surveillance footage

Stored encrypted on‑premise or in EU cloud. Used exclusively for security & incident verification. Retained max. 30 days unless legal hold.

Account credentials

Email, hashed password, multi‑factor identifiers. Never shared. Used for authentication & support ticketing.

Device telemetry

Camera health, connection logs, firmware versions — to diagnose offline issues (see quick fix guide). Aggregated analytics only.

Billing data

Processed via PCI‑DSS compliant partners. AlliTrack never stores full payment details.

Video privacy masking

Privacy zones can be configured directly in camera firmware to block recording of sensitive areas (neighboring windows, public walkways).

Cloud retention

By default, cloud clips are deleted after 14 days. Extended retention requires explicit admin consent and audit‑logging.

Your rights & choices
◉ Right to access Request export of your footage metadata or account data via support ticket.
◉ Right to deletion Surveillance footage older than 30d auto‑deleted. Account deletion erases personal identifiers within 7 days.
◉ Opt out of analytics Disable "improve program" in account settings – no telemetry collected.
◉ Cookie consent Strictly necessary cookies only for login. No tracking cookies without consent.
Privacy requests: privacy@allitrack.eu · response within 48h (GDPR Art. 12)
Limited data sharing
  • 1 Sub‑processors – hosting (AWS eu‑west‑1), ticketing (Zendesk), payment (Stripe). All covered by DPA.
  • 2 Legal disclosures – only if required by law or to protect vital interests (art. 6(1)(c) GDPR).
  • 3 No selling – AlliTrack never sells or rents personal data. Zero third‑party marketing lists.
  • 4 Cross‑border transfer – Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) in place for non‑EEA transfers.

Encryption at rest & transit

All video streams (RTSP/HTTPS) encrypted with TLS 1.3. Footage stored on NAS with AES‑256.

Access control

Role‑based permissions (admin, viewer, auditor). MFA enforced for admin accounts.
