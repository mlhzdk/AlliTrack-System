BUSINESS ALLIANCE INC.

About our company

BUSINESS ALLIANCE INC. is an IT company engaged in providing business solutions in the fields of telecommunications, Voice Over Internet Protocol (VoIP), System Integration, Door Access/Biometrics, IP Surveillance, providing Security Solutions and similar to any kind of business. The company has already established clients and strong partnership with its suppliers. The company is focused in providing unique security and telecommunication solutions through adapting new and state of the art technology that will enhance, upgrade and replace traditional PABX and existing CCTV System in the most economical approach.

Our Mission

  • To provide high quality products and services with reasonable pricing and establishing a successful relationship with our customers and suppliers.
  • To innovate and expand our product lines with new, technologically advanced and state-of-the-art features that will cater all the needs to any kind and size of business as one stop shop provider.
  • To maintain quality products and services to meet the critical challenge in establishing a stronghold in the industry by continuous research and development in the field of telecommunication in a cost effective approach.

Our Vision

  • To be recognized as the leading one stop shop for business solutions provider to any Filipino Enterprise providing high quality in an economical approach and innovative telecommunication services, unmatched support and quality services in terms of supplies to our customers, working in partnership with the team of highly competent and honest individual to meet total customer satisfaction.
