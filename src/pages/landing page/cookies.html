COOKIE NOTICE v1.4 AlliTrack intelligent surveillance Updated 15 March 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how AlliTrack uses cookies and similar technologies on our websites, applications, and support portals. It complements our Privacy Policy and helps you make informed choices.

What are cookies?

Cookies

Small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They remember your preferences, login status, and help us improve your experience.

Similar technologies

We also use local storage, pixels, and session storage for authentication, security, and performance monitoring across our surveillance platforms.

Types of cookies we use

Essential

Required for basic functionality: login sessions, security tokens, and load balancing. Cannot be disabled.

alliTrack_session, XSRF-TOKEN, AWSALB

Analytics

Optional

Help us understand how visitors interact with our documentation and support center. Aggregated, anonymized data.

_ga, _gid, _hjSession

Marketing

Optional

Used to deliver relevant content about AlliTrack solutions. Only with explicit consent.
