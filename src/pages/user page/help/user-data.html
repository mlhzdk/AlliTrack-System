Data Processing Agreement

Last Updated: January 2025

Important: This Data Processing Agreement (DPA) outlines how AlliTrack processes and protects personal data on behalf of our customers. By using our Services, you agree to the terms outlined in this DPA.

1. Agreement Overview

This Data Processing Agreement (DPA) forms part of the AlliTrack Terms of Service and governs the processing of personal data that you upload or provide to AlliTrack in connection with the Services.

2. Definitions

"Data Controller" means the entity which determines the purposes and means of the processing of Personal Data.

"Data Processor" means the entity which processes Personal Data on behalf of the Data Controller.

"Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

"Processing" means any operation performed on Personal Data.

"Data Subject" means the individual to whom Personal Data relates.

3. Roles and Responsibilities

3.1 Data Controller: You, as the user of AlliTrack services, act as the Data Controller for any Personal Data you submit to our Services.

3.2 Data Processor: AlliTrack acts as the Data Processor for Personal Data processed through our Services.

3.3 Processing Instructions: AlliTrack will only process Personal Data in accordance with your documented instructions unless required by law.

4. Data Processing Details

Subject Matter: Provision of ticketing system and customer support services

Duration: For the term of your subscription to AlliTrack services

Nature and Purpose: Processing necessary to provide the Services as described in the Terms of Service

Types of Personal Data: Name, email address, contact information, support ticket content, and other data submitted through the Services

Categories of Data Subjects: Your customers, employees, and other individuals about whom you submit data to the Services

5. Technical and Organizational Measures

AlliTrack implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Physical security of data center facilities
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures
  • Regular staff training on data protection

6. Subprocessing

6.1 Authorization: You provide general authorization for AlliTrack to engage sub-processors to support the delivery of our Services.

6.2 Requirements: AlliTrack will ensure that any sub-processor we engage is bound by data protection obligations no less protective than those in this DPA.

6.3 Current Sub-processors: A current list of sub-processors is available upon request and includes our hosting provider and infrastructure services.

6.4 Notification: We will notify you of any intended changes concerning the addition or replacement of sub-processors, giving you the opportunity to object.

7. Data Subject Rights

7.1 Assistance: AlliTrack will provide reasonable assistance to you in fulfilling your obligations to respond to requests from Data Subjects to exercise their rights under applicable data protection laws.

7.2 Response to Requests: If we receive a request from a Data Subject in relation to their Personal Data, we will direct that individual to you as the Data Controller.

8. Data Breach Notification

8.1 Notification: In the event of a Personal Data breach, AlliTrack will notify you without undue delay after becoming aware of the breach.

8.2 Assistance: AlliTrack will provide reasonable assistance to you in meeting your data breach notification obligations under applicable data protection laws.

8.3 Details: Our notification will include, to the extent possible, the nature of the breach, the categories and approximate number of Data Subjects concerned, and the measures taken or proposed to address the breach.

9. Data Transfer

9.1 International Transfers: AlliTrack may transfer and process Personal Data to countries outside of your country of residence. We will ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

9.2 Safeguards: Where Personal Data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, we will implement appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law.

10. Data Return and Deletion

Upon termination of your account, AlliTrack will, at your choice, delete or return all Personal Data processed on your behalf, and delete existing copies unless applicable law requires storage of the Personal Data.

11. Audit Rights

You have the right to audit AlliTrack's compliance with this DPA. Any audit will be conducted with reasonable prior notice, during normal business hours, and will not interfere with AlliTrack's business activities.
