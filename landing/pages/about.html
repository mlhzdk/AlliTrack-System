Business Alliance Inc.

BUSINESS ALLIANCE INC. is an IT company engaged in providing comprehensive business solutions in telecommunications, Voice Over Internet Protocol (VoIP), System Integration, Door Access/Biometrics, IP Surveillance, and Security Solutions for businesses of all sizes.

With established clients and strong supplier partnerships, we focus on delivering unique security and telecommunication solutions through state-of-the-art technology, enhancing and upgrading traditional systems in the most economical approach.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

  • To provide high quality products and services with reasonable pricing and establishing successful relationships with customers and suppliers.
  • To innovate and expand our product lines with new, technologically advanced features that cater to all business needs as a one-stop shop provider.
  • To maintain quality products and services through continuous research and development in telecommunications with a cost-effective approach.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading one-stop shop for business solutions provider to any Filipino Enterprise, offering high-quality, economical, and innovative telecommunication services with unmatched support and quality supplies, working with a team of highly competent and honest individuals to achieve total customer satisfaction.
